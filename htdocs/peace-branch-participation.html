Welcome to Peace-Branch.
This is just the beginning.

Please enter your userID and password to log in, or click "sign-up" to register a new account.

peace-branch.org allows us to connect with each other around the world, whether far away, or close to home.

This web set only ever transmits your personal data over secure HTTPS.

Peace Branch is about living the way of truth and love, and encouraging other people to do the same. It is also about not having to do so alone. By signing-up, we can, if we wish:

    be notified:
  • when other people near by sign up
  • of local meetings or events
    let others be notified:
  • when we sign-up
  • of meetings or events we're planning
    find out Peace Branch people who:
  • live near us
  • share common interests

This web-site will NOT pass on our information to any third party for any purposes, except if and when it is required to do so by law.

The purposes of keeping personal data are for putting us in touch with one another and publicising events, and for producing general statistics, such as the number of people signed-up in a given country.
